Chelsea Football Club(1905-present)

This is a small profile on Chelsea F.C

History

Chelsea was founded on March 10 1905 at the Rising Sun Pub (now The Butcher's Hook).Its first manager was former Arsenal player Ted Drake who is credited for a lot of success in the club In June 2003 the club was sold to a Russian billionaire
Roman Abramovich.

Stadium

Chelsea have had only one home ground since inception,Stamford Bridge.In order to keep pace with other competitive clubs it was suggested that the club build a new stadium and on January 2017,the club got approval from the town council to build one.

Rivalries

Chelsea have long standing rivalries with Arsenal and Tottenham Hotspur.However a survey done in 2012 shows that Chelsea consider Tottenham as their greatest rival.
